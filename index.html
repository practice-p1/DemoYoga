Our philosophy

Our story

Where ancient wisdom meets modern life.

Saṃskāra was born from a simple belief: that yoga belongs to everyone. Founded in 2015 in Banjara Hills, we've grown from a small studio with 3 teachers to a thriving community of 500+ members and 12 certified instructors.

The word Saṃskāra means "impression" or "transformation" in Sanskrit — a reminder that every practice, every breath, every moment of stillness leaves a mark on who we become.

What guides us

Our core values

01
Accessibility

Yoga belongs to every body. We offer classes at all levels, adaptive modifications, and a welcoming environment where no one feels out of place.

02
Authenticity

We honour the roots of yoga — its philosophy, its Sanskrit, its depth — while making it relevant and accessible for contemporary life in India and beyond.

03
Community

The studio is a gathering place. We cultivate relationships, shared journeys, and a sense of belonging that extends far beyond the mat and the class.
