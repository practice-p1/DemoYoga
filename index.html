Why Yoga?

What yoga does
for your body & mind

Every class at PranaDhara is designed to deliver real, lasting benefits — physically, mentally, and spiritually.

🤸
Enhances Flexibility

Regular practice gradually loosens muscles and connective tissue, improving your range of motion and ease of movement in daily life.

🛡️
Boosts Immunity

Yoga stimulates the lymphatic system, reduces cortisol, and strengthens the body's natural defence mechanisms from within.

💪
Builds Strength

Many poses require supporting your own bodyweight in new ways, building functional strength across every major muscle group.

⚖️
Weight Management

Active yoga burns calories, builds lean muscle, and brings mindful awareness to eating habits — a holistic approach to a healthy weight.

⚡
Increases Energy Levels

Breathwork and movement unlock dormant energy, reduce fatigue, and leave you feeling refreshed and vibrant throughout the day.

🌿
Supports Digestive Health

Twisting and compressing poses massage the digestive organs, promoting gut health, reducing bloating, and improving metabolism.

Our Story

Bringing ancient yoga to every home in Bangalore.

PranaDhara YogaKendra was founded with one heartfelt mission — to make authentic, transformative yoga accessible to every woman and child in our community.

Located in the heart of Marathahalii, our studio provides a safe, nurturing, and joyful space where students of all ages and experience levels discover the profound benefits of yoga.

Under the guidance of Vijayashree, our certified yoga instructor, we offer both in-studio and online sessions designed to fit every schedule and lifestyle.
